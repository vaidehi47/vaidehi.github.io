Project

The healthcare app is designed to facilitate seamless communication between patients and healthcare professionals, allowing patients to easily reach out to doctors for consultations, advice, and follow-up care. By offering a user-friendly interface, the app ensures timely and efficient access to medical support, enhancing patient convenience and healthcare delivery.

"Connect with Doctors Anytime, Anywhere"

The app enables patients to schedule virtual consultations, access medical records, and receive personalized health recommendations, improving overall healthcare accessibility and patient experience.

Real-Time Doctor Availability and Consultancy Timings

Comprehensive, live data regarding the availability of doctors, including their consultation timings and the specific locations or healthcare facilities they attend, is essential.

Live Bed, ICU, and NICU Availability Data

Real-time data on the availability of hospital beds, ICU, and NICU across various healthcare facilities should be continuously monitored and updated. This would provide critical insights for patients and healthcare professionals, ensuring informed decisions in times of medical need.

Accurate and up-to-date information on doctor visits to different regions, including medical camps, outreach programs, and other healthcare initiatives, is important for patients in underserved or remote areas.
